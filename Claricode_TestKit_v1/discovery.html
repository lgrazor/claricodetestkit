Discovery Phase

This is the initial phase of any software project. On the testing side, there are a number of scenarios in which you can enter a project. It could be a legacy system, pre-development, or mid development.

No matter the scenario, the goal will remain the same, at the end of this phase you will want to have a solid understanding of the project.

Before your team begins working on a new project or application, there will hopefully be some sort of "kickstart" meeting. No matter what your team may call it, it is a good forum to ask key questions.
