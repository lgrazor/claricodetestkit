Reporting Phase

Communicating the issue to developers and stakeholders is the most crucial task of our position. How we go about notifying the dev team can save money, time, and energy.

The techniques you use to capture an issue will define how successful of a tester you are. This efficiency carries over, and allows the development team the opportunity build more features instead of fixing ones.
